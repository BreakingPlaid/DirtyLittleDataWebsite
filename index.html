Our Origin Story

While working together at GE Aerospace, we bonded over work ethic, passion for data, and rebellion against the guys in plaid shirts—and "Breaking Plaid" was born. Unfortuantely the "Breaking Plaid" domain name would be taken, but we have plenty of puns, and found "Dirty Little Data" too perfect to pass up. We are dedicated to learning and teaching using clear language and practical examples.
